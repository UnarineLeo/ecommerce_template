Why Unipply Exists

South African students often repeat the same steps across multiple university websites. We built Unipply to simplify discovery and help students compare institutions and programs in one place.

One Place to Discover

Unipply brings together university information, locations, and key details so students can explore options faster.

Program Visibility

We list popular study fields and courses to help students identify institutions that align with their interests.

Application Clarity

We surface useful deadline and university information to make planning easier and reduce confusion.

How Applications Work on Unipply

Unipply is a central discovery and guidance platform. We do not submit final applications directly on behalf of universities.

1

Search universities and compare available programs.

2

Review key details such as location, focus areas, and timelines.

3

Proceed to the relevant university channels to complete official applications.
